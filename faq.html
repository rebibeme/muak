Where can I request official receipt?

Seekseek's official reciepts are usually attached with the item with parcel. However, if you did not recieve the reciept with the parcel, please send your request to seekseek@gmail.com

The official reciept will be sent to you via email.



How do I know if my payment was successful?

You will see a 'Payment Succesful' icon appear at the order received page once you have completed the payment process.

You will also receive a order confirming notification to your registered email.






What are the conditions for a Return & Refund Request?

Return and refund are only applicable to the following reasons:
  • I did not receive the product(s) (e.g. missing item, parcel lost in transit)
  • I received incomplete product(s) (e.g. incomplete order)
  • I received the wrong product(s) (e.g. wrong item, wrong size)
  • I received product(s) with physical damage (e.g. broken, cracked cookies)
  • I received faulty product(s) (e.g. malfunctioned, food not fresh)
Return and refund are NOT applicable to the following reasons:
  • I changed my mind
  • I bought wrong size (e.g. incorrect size selected during purchasing)

Return Terms and Conditions

  • Return request must be done within 2 days after arrival.
  • Product must be in the same condition as received.
